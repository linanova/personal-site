about me
Let's talk !

I love computer science because of the way it blends logic and creativity. I take joy in solving puzzles while maintaining artistic freedom - which tools to use, how to put them together, what approach to take.

Through my course studies I developed resourcefulness and an appreciation for continuous learning. At work terms with IBM, Kardium, and Perforce I felt a sense of ownership and pride working on real products. Now, as a recent graduate, I’m looking to prove my skills and have fun building great software.

I am interested in low-level concepts such as pipelining, interprocess communication, and memory management. I am fascinated by software security and computer vision. I also enjoy playing with modern web frameworks and the magic that is JavaScript.

Outside of the computer world I love art, books, and astronomy, and I’m always thinking about my next adventure!
